Use this calculator to estimate PayPal fees and the net amount you will actually receive.

## Formula

**Fee = (Payment Amount × Fee %) + Fixed Fee**

**Net Amount = Payment Amount - Fee**

## Example

If the payment amount is **500**, the fee is **3.49%**, and the fixed fee is **0.49**:

- Fee = 17.94
- Net Amount = 482.06

## Why it matters

PayPal convenience often comes with higher fees. This calculator helps you understand the true cost.

## Common use cases

- Freelance payments
- Ecommerce orders
- Marketplace selling
- International payment planning
