Use this calculator to estimate Stripe payment fees and your final payout after processing costs.

## Formula

**Fee = (Payment Amount × Fee %) + Fixed Fee**

**Net Payout = Payment Amount - Fee**

## Example

If the payment amount is **500**, the fee is **2.9%**, and the fixed fee is **0.30**:

- Fee = 14.80
- Net Payout = 485.20

## Why it matters

Stripe fees affect cash flow, profitability, and pricing strategy. This calculator makes those costs visible.

- Ecommerce pricing
- Invoice planning
- SaaS payment forecasting
