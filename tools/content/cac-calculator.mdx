Use this calculator to work out customer acquisition cost based on total spend and new customers acquired.

## Formula

**CAC = Total Marketing Spend ÷ New Customers**

## Example

If you spent **3000** and acquired **60** customers:

- CAC = 50.00

## Why it matters

CAC is one of the most important metrics in growth marketing. It helps you understand the real cost of acquiring customers.

## Common use cases

- Paid acquisition analysis
- Startup unit economics
- Budget planning
- Profitability checks
