Use this calculator to estimate customer acquisition cost based on marketing spend and customers acquired.

## What this calculator does

The CAC calculator helps you determine how much it costs to acquire a new customer.

It compares:

- total marketing and sales spend
- number of customers acquired

## CAC Formula

**Customer Acquisition Cost = Total Marketing Spend ÷ Number of Customers Acquired**

## Example calculation

If:

- Marketing spend = **5000**
- Customers acquired = **100**

Then:

CAC = **5000 ÷ 100**

CAC = **50**

## What is customer acquisition cost?

Customer acquisition cost (CAC) measures how much money a business spends to acquire a new customer.

It includes:

- advertising costs
- marketing tools
- sales expenses
- campaign costs

## Why CAC matters

CAC helps businesses:

- measure marketing efficiency
- evaluate campaign performance
- improve profitability
- balance growth and spending
