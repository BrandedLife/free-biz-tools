Use this calculator to work out what percentage one number is of another.

## What this calculator does

The percentage calculator helps you find the percentage that a part represents of a whole.

It uses:

- part value
- whole value

This gives you the percentage result.

## Percentage Formula

**Percentage = (Part ÷ Whole) × 100**

Where:

- **Part** = the smaller value or portion
- **Whole** = the total value
- **Percentage** = the proportion expressed as a percentage

## Example calculation

If:

- Part = **25**
- Whole = **200**

Then:

- Percentage = **25 ÷ 200 × 100**
- Percentage = **12.5%**

## What is a percentage?

A percentage expresses a number as a portion of 100.

It is commonly used to compare values, measure changes, and understand proportions.

## Why percentages matter

Percentages help you understand:

- what share something represents
- how large or small a result is relative to a total
- comparisons between values
- discounts, margins, growth, and conversion metrics

## When to use this calculator

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- putting the numbers in the wrong order
- dividing by the wrong total
- confusing percentage with percentage change
- forgetting that the whole value cannot be zero

## Percentage vs percentage change

These are different.

- **Percentage** tells you what part of a whole something represents
- **Percentage change** tells you how much a value increased or decreased compared to an earlier value

## FAQs

### How do you calculate a percentage?

Percentage = (Part ÷ Whole) × 100

### What does percentage mean?

Percentage shows how much one value represents out of a total of 100.

### Why is percentage useful?

It makes proportions easier to compare and understand.

### Can the whole be zero?

No. You cannot divide by zero, so the whole value must not be zero.
