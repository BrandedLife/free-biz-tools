# Profit Margin Calculator

Use this calculator to work out your profit margin based on revenue and cost.

## Formula

**Profit Margin (%) = ((Revenue - Cost) / Revenue) × 100**

## Example

If your revenue is **1000** and your cost is **700**:

- Profit = 300
- Profit Margin = 30%

## Why it matters

Profit margin helps you understand how much of each sale you actually keep after costs.

## Common use cases

- Pricing products
- Planning discounts
- Comparing suppliers
- Checking whether a product line is worth keeping
