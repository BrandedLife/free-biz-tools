Use this calculator to estimate return on investment based on profit and investment cost.

## What this calculator does

The ROI calculator helps you measure how profitable an investment or project is relative to its cost.

It compares:

- total profit
- total investment

## ROI Formula

**ROI (%) = ((Profit − Investment Cost) ÷ Investment Cost) × 100**

## Example calculation

If:

- Investment cost = **5000**
- Profit generated = **7500**

Then:

Net profit = **2500**

ROI = **(2500 ÷ 5000) × 100**

ROI = **50%**

## What is ROI?

Return on investment (ROI) measures the efficiency of an investment.

It shows how much return you generate relative to what you invested.

## Why ROI matters

ROI helps businesses:

- evaluate marketing campaigns
- compare investment opportunities
- measure business performance
- prioritize profitable projects

## When to use this calculator

Use this calculator when you want to:

- evaluate investments
- measure marketing effectiveness
- compare business opportunities
- review financial performance

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- ignoring indirect costs
- using inaccurate revenue figures
- measuring ROI over the wrong time period

## ROI vs profit

Profit shows the amount earned.

ROI shows how efficiently money was invested to generate that profit.
