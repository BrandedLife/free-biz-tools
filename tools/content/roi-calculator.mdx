Use this calculator to estimate return on investment based on gain and cost.

## Formula

**ROI (%) = ((Gain - Cost) ÷ Cost) × 100**

## Example

If the gain is **15000** and the cost is **10000**:

- Profit = 5000
- ROI = 50.00%

## Why it matters

ROI helps you compare the performance of different investments, campaigns, or projects.

## Common use cases

- Marketing analysis
- Investment evaluation
- Project performance reviews
- Business decision-making
