## What this calculator does

The revenue calculator helps you determine how much income a business generates from sales.

It uses:

- selling price
- quantity sold

This provides a quick estimate of total revenue.

## Revenue Formula

**Revenue = Price × Quantity**

Where:

- **Price** = selling price per unit
- **Quantity** = number of units sold

## Example calculation

If:

- Price per unit = **50**
- Quantity sold = **200**

Then:

Revenue = **50 × 200**

Revenue = **10000**

## What is revenue?

Revenue is the total income generated from selling products or services before any costs are deducted.

It is sometimes referred to as **sales** or **top-line revenue**.

## Why revenue matters

Revenue helps businesses:

- measure business growth
- evaluate product performance
- forecast sales targets
- attract investors

Revenue alone does not indicate profitability, but it is an essential performance indicator.

## When to use this calculator

Use this calculator when you want to:

- estimate income from product sales
- forecast future revenue
- compare pricing strategies
- evaluate marketing performance

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes when estimating revenue include:

- ignoring discounts or refunds
- confusing revenue with profit
- using unrealistic sales assumptions
- excluding transaction fees or taxes

## Revenue vs profit

Revenue and profit are different metrics.

- **Revenue** is total sales income
- **Profit** is revenue minus costs

A company can have strong revenue but still be unprofitable.
