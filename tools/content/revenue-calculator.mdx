Use this calculator to work out total revenue based on units sold and selling price.

## Formula

**Revenue = Units Sold × Price Per Unit**

## Example

If you sell **250** units at **40** each:

- Revenue = 10000

## Why it matters

Revenue is the starting point for understanding business performance. It helps you forecast income and compare sales scenarios.

## Common use cases

- Sales forecasting
- Offer planning
- Product sales analysis
- Pricing scenario testing
