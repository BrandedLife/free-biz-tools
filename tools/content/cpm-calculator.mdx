Use this calculator to estimate cost per thousand impressions in advertising campaigns.

## What this calculator does

The CPM calculator measures how much you pay for one thousand ad impressions.

## CPM Formula

**CPM = (Total Advertising Cost ÷ Impressions) × 1000**

## Example calculation

If:

- Advertising cost = **500**
- Impressions = **100000**

Then:

CPM = **(500 ÷ 100000) × 1000**

CPM = **5**

## What is CPM?

CPM stands for **cost per mille**, meaning cost per thousand impressions.

It is widely used in:

- display advertising
- social media advertising
- brand awareness campaigns

## Why CPM matters

CPM helps advertisers:

- evaluate brand awareness campaigns
- compare advertising platforms
- estimate campaign reach
