Use this calculator to work out cost per thousand impressions based on spend and impressions.

## Formula

**CPM = (Ad Spend ÷ Impressions) × 1000**

## Example

If spend is **400** and impressions are **50000**:

- CPM = 8.00

## Why it matters

CPM helps you compare awareness campaigns and understand the cost of reaching audiences at scale.

## Common use cases

- Display ad reporting
- Brand awareness campaigns
- Social media ads
- Media buying comparisons
