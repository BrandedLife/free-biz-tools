## What are Amazon FBA fees?

Amazon FBA fees are the charges sellers pay to use Amazon’s fulfillment and marketplace services.

These often include referral fees and fulfillment fees, both of which directly affect margin.

## Why Amazon fees matter

Amazon fees matter because they affect:

- product pricing
- net revenue
- margin per sale
- viability of a product line

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- ignoring referral fees
- forgetting fulfillment charges
- pricing too low to absorb fees
- comparing Amazon pricing to direct-store pricing without adjustment

## Amazon FBA fees vs Shopify fees

These are very different fee structures.

- **Amazon FBA fees** are tied to Amazon’s marketplace and fulfillment model
- **Shopify fees** are usually tied to platform and payment processing costs

## FAQs

### What are Amazon FBA fees?

Amazon FBA fees include marketplace referral fees and fulfillment-related charges.

### Why should you calculate Amazon FBA fees?

Because fees directly affect product margin and overall profitability.

### What affects Amazon FBA fees?

Sale price, category, size, weight, and fulfillment structure can all affect fees.

### What is net revenue after Amazon FBA fees?

Net revenue is the amount left after Amazon-related fees are deducted from the sale price.
