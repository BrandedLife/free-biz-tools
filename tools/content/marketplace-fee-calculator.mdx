## What this calculator does

The marketplace fee calculator helps estimate the fee charged by a marketplace and the amount you keep after fees.

It is useful for:

- pricing analysis
- channel planning
- margin review
- marketplace comparisons

## Formula

**Marketplace Fee = Sale Price × Fee Percentage**

Where:

- **Sale Price** = total product sale amount
- **Fee Percentage** = marketplace commission rate
- **Marketplace Fee** = fee charged by the marketplace

## Example calculation

If:

- Sale price = **1000**
- Marketplace fee = **15%**

Then:

- Marketplace fee = **1000 × 0.15**
- Marketplace fee = **150**
- Net amount = **850**

## What is a marketplace fee?

A marketplace fee is the amount a platform charges for facilitating a sale.

## Why marketplace fees matter

This calculation helps businesses:

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- ignoring other platform fees
- forgetting payment processing fees
- using the wrong commission rate
- comparing net payouts without shipping context

## Marketplace fee vs net amount

These are closely related.

- **Marketplace fee** shows what the platform keeps
- **Net amount** shows what you keep after fees
