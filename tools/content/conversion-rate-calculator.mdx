Use this calculator to estimate conversion rate based on visitors and conversions.

## What this calculator does

The conversion rate calculator measures how many visitors take a desired action on your website.

## Conversion Rate Formula

**Conversion Rate (%) = (Conversions ÷ Visitors) × 100**

## Example calculation

If:

- Visitors = **1000**
- Conversions = **50**

Then:

Conversion Rate = **(50 ÷ 1000) × 100**

Conversion Rate = **5%**

## What is conversion rate?

Conversion rate measures the percentage of users who complete a desired action.

Examples include:

- purchases
- form submissions
- signups
- downloads
