Use this calculator to calculate conversion rate based on visitors and conversions.

## Formula

**Conversion Rate (%) = (Conversions ÷ Visitors) × 100**

## Example

If you had **1000** visitors and **25** conversions:

- Conversion Rate = 2.50%

## Why it matters

Conversion rate helps you understand how effectively your traffic turns into leads or sales.

## Common use cases

- Landing page analysis
- Ecommerce optimization
- Funnel reviews
- Ad traffic performance checks
