# Markup Calculator

Use this calculator to work out markup percentage based on cost and selling price.

## Formula

**Markup (%) = ((Selling Price - Cost) / Cost) × 100**

## Example

If your cost is **500** and your selling price is **750**:

- Profit = 250
- Markup = 50%

## Why it matters

Markup helps you decide how much to add to your cost price when setting selling prices.

## Common use cases

- Setting retail prices
- Pricing wholesale products
- Protecting margins
- Testing different pricing strategies
