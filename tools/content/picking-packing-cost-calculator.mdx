## What this calculator does

The picking and packing cost calculator helps estimate the total cost of pick-and-pack activity.

It is useful for:

- warehouse analysis
- fulfilment costing
- process planning
- operational benchmarking

## Formula

**Picking and Packing Cost = Orders Picked and Packed × Pick and Pack Cost Per Order**

Where:

- **Orders Picked and Packed** = number of orders processed
- **Pick and Pack Cost Per Order** = average cost for each order
- **Picking and Packing Cost** = total pick-and-pack cost

## Example calculation

If:

- Orders picked and packed = **1800**
- Pick and pack cost per order = **12**

Then:

- Picking and packing cost = **1800 × 12**
- Picking and packing cost = **21600**

## What is picking and packing cost?

Picking and packing cost is the total cost of preparing orders for shipment.

## Why picking and packing cost matters

This calculation helps businesses:

- understand warehouse efficiency

- compare internal and outsourced operations
- improve cost control

Use this calculator when you want to:

- review operational costs

- compare fulfilment models

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- excluding packaging materials
- underestimating labour cost
- mixing order types with different complexity
- assuming all orders cost the same

## Picking and packing cost vs fulfillment cost

These are closely related.

- **Picking and packing cost** is a specific operational cost
- **Fulfillment cost** is a broader end-to-end order processing cost
