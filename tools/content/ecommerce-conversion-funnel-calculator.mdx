Use this calculator to estimate ecommerce conversion rate.

## What this calculator does

The ecommerce conversion funnel calculator helps measure how many store sessions become completed orders.

It is useful for:

- store analysis
- conversion tracking
- campaign review
- ecommerce forecasting

## Formula

**Ecommerce Conversion Rate = Orders ÷ Sessions × 100**

Where:

- **Orders** = completed purchases
- **Sessions** = total store visits
- **Ecommerce Conversion Rate** = percentage of sessions that become orders

## Example calculation

If:

- Orders = **180**
- Sessions = **6000**

Then:

- Ecommerce conversion rate = **180 ÷ 6000 × 100**
- Ecommerce conversion rate = **3%**

## What is ecommerce conversion rate?

Ecommerce conversion rate is the percentage of store sessions that turn into orders.

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- mixing sessions and users
- using inconsistent date ranges
- comparing very different traffic sources
- ignoring mobile and desktop differences

## Ecommerce conversion rate vs cart abandonment rate

These are closely related.

- **Ecommerce conversion rate** shows completed orders as a percentage of sessions
- **Cart abandonment rate** shows how many created carts do not become orders
