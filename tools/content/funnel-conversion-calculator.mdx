## What this calculator does

The funnel conversion calculator helps measure how many top-of-funnel visitors become final conversions.

It is useful for:

- funnel analysis
- campaign planning
- conversion tracking
- performance improvement

## Formula

**Funnel Conversion Rate = Final Conversions ÷ Top-of-Funnel Visitors × 100**

Where:

- **Final Conversions** = completed purchases, signups, or actions
- **Top-of-Funnel Visitors** = users entering the funnel
- **Funnel Conversion Rate** = percentage of visitors who convert

## Example calculation

If:

- Final conversions = **120**
- Top-of-funnel visitors = **4000**

Then:

- Funnel conversion rate = **120 ÷ 4000 × 100**
- Funnel conversion rate = **3%**

## What is funnel conversion rate?

Funnel conversion rate is the percentage of initial visitors who complete the final desired action.

## Why funnel conversion rate matters

This calculation helps businesses:

- identify weak funnel stages
- improve conversion performance
- benchmark results
- support forecasting

## When to use this calculator

Use this calculator when you want to:

- review conversion funnels
- compare landing pages or campaigns
- estimate sales efficiency
- improve user journeys

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- tracking the wrong conversion goal
- using inconsistent traffic windows
- ignoring drop-off between stages
- comparing funnels with different audiences

## Funnel conversion rate vs landing page conversion rate

These are closely related.

- **Funnel conversion rate** measures the whole journey
- **Landing page conversion rate** measures one page only
