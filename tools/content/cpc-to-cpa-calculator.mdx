## What this calculator does

The CPC to CPA calculator helps estimate how much each acquisition costs based on click cost and conversion rate.

It is useful for:

- acquisition forecasting
- campaign planning
- paid media analysis
- budget modeling

## CPC to CPA Formula

**CPA = CPC ÷ Conversion Rate**

Where:

- **CPC** = cost per click
- **Conversion Rate** = percentage of clicks that convert
- **CPA** = cost per acquisition

## Example calculation

If:

- CPC = **2**
- Conversion rate = **4%**

Then:

- CPA = **2 ÷ 0.04**
- CPA = **50**

## What is CPA?

CPA stands for cost per acquisition.

It measures how much it costs to generate one conversion, sale, or lead.

## Why CPA matters

CPA helps marketers:

- estimate acquisition cost
- compare channels
- understand profitability
- improve budget allocation

## When to use this calculator

Use this calculator when you want to:

- forecast lead or sale costs
- compare campaign performance
- estimate acquisition economics
- improve paid traffic planning

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- using a low-quality conversion rate estimate
- forgetting that landing page quality affects CPA
- comparing CPA across very different funnel stages

## CPC vs CPA

These metrics are linked but different.

- **CPC** measures click cost
- **CPA** measures acquisition cost

A campaign can have a low CPC but still have a poor CPA if conversion rate is weak.
