## What this calculator does

The TikTok Shop fee calculator helps estimate platform fees and the amount left after those fees.

It is useful for:

- channel comparison
- pricing analysis
- payout estimation
- margin review

## Formula

**TikTok Shop Fee = Sale Price × Fee Percentage**

Where:

- **Sale Price** = total sale amount
- **Fee Percentage** = TikTok Shop commission rate
- **TikTok Shop Fee** = estimated platform fee

## Example calculation

If:

- Sale price = **1000**
- TikTok Shop fee = **8%**

Then:

- TikTok Shop fee = **1000 × 0.08**
- TikTok Shop fee = **80**
- Net amount = **920**
