Use this calculator to work out the discount amount and final price after applying a percentage discount.

## Formula

**Discount Amount = Original Price × Discount %**

**Final Price = Original Price - Discount Amount**

## Example

If the original price is **1000** and the discount is **20%**:

- Discount Amount = 200
- Final Price = 800

## Why it matters

Discounting can increase sales, but it also affects revenue and profitability. This calculator helps you understand the actual impact.

## Common use cases

- Running promotions
- Checking markdown prices
- Planning ecommerce sales
- Comparing discount scenarios
