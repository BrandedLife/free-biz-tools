Use this calculator to work out return on ad spend based on revenue and advertising costs.

## Formula

**ROAS = Revenue ÷ Ad Spend**

## Example

If revenue is **5000** and ad spend is **1000**:

- ROAS = 5.00x

## Why it matters

ROAS helps you understand whether your advertising is producing enough revenue relative to spend.

## Common use cases

- Meta ads analysis
- Google Ads reporting
- Ecommerce campaign reviews
- Budget allocation decisions
