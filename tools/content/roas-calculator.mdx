Use this calculator to estimate return on ad spend based on revenue and advertising costs.

## What this calculator does

The ROAS calculator measures the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

## ROAS Formula

**ROAS = Revenue from Ads ÷ Advertising Cost**

## Example calculation

If:

- Revenue from ads = **5000**
- Advertising cost = **1000**

Then:

ROAS = **5000 ÷ 1000**

ROAS = **5**

## What is ROAS?

Return on ad spend (ROAS) measures how much revenue is generated for every dollar spent on advertising.

## Why ROAS matters

ROAS helps marketers:

- measure campaign profitability
- allocate advertising budgets
- optimize marketing channels
