Use this calculator to estimate ARPA.

## What this calculator does

The ARPA calculator helps measure average revenue per account.

It is useful for:

- SaaS analysis
- account benchmarking
- pricing review
- revenue planning

## Formula

**ARPA = Monthly Recurring Revenue ÷ Active Accounts**

Where:

- **Monthly Recurring Revenue** = recurring monthly revenue
- **Active Accounts** = number of active customer accounts
- **ARPA** = average revenue per account

## Example calculation

If:

- Monthly recurring revenue = **50000**
- Active accounts = **500**

Then:

- ARPA = **50000 ÷ 500**
- ARPA = **100**

## What is ARPA?

ARPA is the average recurring revenue generated per account.

## Why ARPA matters

This calculation helps businesses:

- understand account-level monetization
- benchmark pricing tiers
- assess account expansion opportunities
- support revenue forecasting

## When to use this calculator

Use this calculator when you want to:

- review revenue per account
- compare plans or segments
- support pricing decisions
- benchmark account performance

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- confusing users with accounts
- mixing recurring and one-time revenue
- using inconsistent active account definitions
- comparing periods inconsistently

## ARPA vs ARPU

These are closely related.

- **ARPA** measures revenue per account
- **ARPU** measures revenue per user
