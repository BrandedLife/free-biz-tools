Use this calculator to calculate profit based on revenue and total costs.

## Formula

**Profit = Revenue - Total Costs**

## Example

If revenue is **5000** and total costs are **3200**:

- Profit = 1800

## Why it matters

Profit is one of the most important numbers in any business. It shows what is left after all relevant costs are covered.

## Common use cases

- Monthly business reviews
- Product line analysis
- Budget planning
- Pricing decisions
