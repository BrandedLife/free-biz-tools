Use this calculator to convert CPM into estimated CPC using click-through rate.

## What this calculator does

The CPM to CPC calculator helps estimate what your cost per click would be based on CPM and CTR.

It is useful for:

- comparing ad models
- forecasting campaign costs
- media planning
- paid traffic analysis

## CPM to CPC Formula

**CPC = CPM ÷ (1000 × CTR)**

Where:

- **CPM** = cost per thousand impressions
- **CTR** = click-through rate expressed as a percentage
- **CPC** = estimated cost per click

## Example calculation

If:

- CPM = **10**
- CTR = **2%**

Then:

- CPC = **10 ÷ (1000 × 0.02)**
- CPC = **0.50**

## What does CPM to CPC mean?

This conversion estimates how much each click costs when your media buying model is based on impressions rather than clicks.

It helps connect awareness pricing with traffic pricing.

This helps advertisers:

- understand performance trade-offs
- choose media buying strategies more intelligently

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- using CTR as a whole number instead of a percentage
- comparing estimated CPC to actual CPC without context
- ignoring creative quality and targeting differences

## CPM vs CPC

These are two different pricing models.

- **CPM** charges for impressions
- **CPC** charges for clicks

This calculator helps translate one into the other for better planning.
