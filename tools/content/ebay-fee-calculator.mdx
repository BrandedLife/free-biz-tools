## What this calculator does

The eBay fee calculator helps estimate eBay fees and the amount left after those fees.

It is useful for:

- eBay pricing
- margin planning
- payout estimation
- channel analysis

## Formula

**eBay Fee = Sale Price × Final Value Fee Percentage**

Where:

- **Sale Price** = product sale amount
- **Final Value Fee Percentage** = eBay final value fee rate
- **eBay Fee** = estimated eBay fee

## Example calculation

If:

- Sale price = **1200**
- Final value fee = **12%**

Then:

- eBay fee = **1200 × 0.12**
- eBay fee = **144**
- Net amount = **1056**

## What are eBay fees?

eBay fees are the platform costs charged on sales made through eBay.

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- using the wrong fee percentage
- forgetting extra fees
- comparing gross sales instead of net payout
- ignoring shipping and fulfilment costs

## eBay fees vs marketplace fees

These are closely related.
