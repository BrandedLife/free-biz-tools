## What this calculator does

The cash runway calculator helps estimate how many months a business can continue operating based on available cash and monthly burn.

It is useful for:

- funding planning
- startup management
- budgeting
- cash risk analysis

## Formula

**Cash Runway = Cash Balance ÷ Monthly Burn**

Where:

- **Cash Balance** = current available cash
- **Monthly Burn** = average monthly cash used
- **Cash Runway** = number of months cash can last

## Example calculation

If:

- Cash balance = **100000**
- Monthly burn = **20000**

Then:

- Cash runway = **100000 ÷ 20000**
- Cash runway = **5**

## What is cash runway?

Cash runway is the number of months a business can continue operating before cash runs out, assuming burn stays constant.

## Why cash runway matters

This calculation helps businesses:

- plan funding needs
- manage risk

- improve financial visibility

Use this calculator when you want to:

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- using inconsistent burn rate estimates
- ignoring expected revenue changes
- assuming burn stays fixed when it may change
- excluding restricted cash

## Cash runway vs burn rate

These are closely related.

- **Cash runway** shows how long cash lasts
- **Burn rate** shows how fast cash is being used
