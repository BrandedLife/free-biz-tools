## What this calculator does

The SaaS LTV:CAC ratio calculator helps compare lifetime value to customer acquisition cost.

It is useful for:

- SaaS benchmarking
- marketing analysis
- growth planning
- unit economics review

## Formula

**LTV:CAC Ratio = LTV ÷ CAC**

Where:

- **LTV** = lifetime value of a customer
- **CAC** = customer acquisition cost
- **LTV:CAC Ratio** = unit economics ratio

## Example calculation

If:

- LTV = **900**
- CAC = **300**

Then:

- LTV:CAC ratio = **900 ÷ 300**
- LTV:CAC ratio = **3.00**

## What is LTV:CAC ratio?

LTV:CAC ratio measures how much customer value is generated for each unit of acquisition cost.

## Why LTV:CAC ratio matters

This calculation helps businesses:

- assess growth efficiency
- compare acquisition strategy
- benchmark SaaS economics
- improve resource allocation

Use this calculator when you want to:

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- using poor LTV estimates
- excluding some acquisition costs
- comparing different customer segments without adjustment
- relying on the ratio alone without churn context

## LTV:CAC vs churn rate

These are closely related.

- **LTV:CAC** measures value relative to acquisition cost
- **Churn rate** affects how long customer value lasts
