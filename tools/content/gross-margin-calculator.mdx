Use this calculator to work out gross margin based on revenue and cost of goods sold.

## Formula

**Gross Margin (%) = ((Revenue - Cost of Goods Sold) / Revenue) × 100**

## Example

If revenue is **1000** and cost of goods sold is **650**:

- Gross Profit = 350
- Gross Margin = 35%

## Why it matters

Gross margin helps you measure how efficiently you generate profit from direct production or product costs.

## Common use cases

- Product profitability analysis
- Pricing reviews
- Supplier comparisons
- Business performance reporting
