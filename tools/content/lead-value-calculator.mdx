Use this calculator to estimate lead value.

## What this calculator does

The lead value calculator helps determine the average value of each lead.

It is useful for:

- lead generation planning
- campaign analysis
- sales forecasting
- budget allocation

## Formula

**Lead Value = Total Revenue ÷ Total Leads**

Where:

- **Total Revenue** = revenue generated from leads
- **Total Leads** = total number of leads acquired
- **Lead Value** = average value per lead

## Example calculation

If:

- Total revenue = **50000**
- Total leads = **250**

Then:

- Lead value = **50000 ÷ 250**
- Lead value = **200**

## What is lead value?

Lead value is the average amount of revenue generated per lead over a defined period.

## Why lead value matters

This calculation helps businesses:

- understand lead quality
- compare marketing channels
- set cost-per-lead targets
- plan budgets more accurately

## When to use this calculator

Use this calculator when you want to:

- value incoming leads
- compare paid and organic traffic
- review campaign efficiency
- improve acquisition strategy

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- using incomplete revenue data
- mixing different time periods
- ignoring lead quality differences
- comparing channels without enough sample size

## Lead value vs cost per lead

These are closely related.

- **Lead value** shows what a lead is worth
- **Cost per lead** shows what it costs to acquire one

## FAQs

### What does this calculator do?

It helps you calculate average revenue per lead.

### Why is lead value important?

It helps you understand how much you can afford to spend to acquire leads.

### Is lead value the same as customer value?

No. Lead value includes all leads, while customer value focuses only on converted customers.
