## What this calculator does

The CPC calculator helps determine the average cost you pay for each click in an advertising campaign.

## CPC Formula

**Cost Per Click = Total Advertising Cost ÷ Number of Clicks**

## Example calculation

If:

- Advertising spend = **1000**
- Clicks = **500**

Then:

CPC = **1000 ÷ 500**

CPC = **2**

## What is cost per click?

Cost per click (CPC) measures how much you pay for each click in a digital advertising campaign.

It is commonly used in:

- Google Ads
- Meta Ads
- LinkedIn Ads
- display advertising

## Why CPC matters

CPC helps marketers:

- measure advertising efficiency
- compare advertising platforms
- optimize campaign performance
- control marketing costs
