Use this calculator to estimate cost per click based on ad spend and total clicks.

## Formula

**CPC = Ad Spend ÷ Clicks**

## Example

If ad spend is **500** and clicks are **250**:

- CPC = 2.00

## Why it matters

CPC helps you understand how expensive traffic is and whether your ad targeting is efficient.

## Common use cases

- Paid search analysis
- Social ad reporting
- Campaign optimization
- Traffic cost planning
