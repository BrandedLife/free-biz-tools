Use this calculator to estimate bundle profit and bundle margin.

## What this calculator does

The product bundle profit calculator helps determine profit and margin on a product bundle.

It is useful for:

- pricing analysis
- bundle planning
- margin review
- promotional strategy

## Formula

**Bundle Profit = Bundle Price - Total Bundle Cost**

Where:

- **Bundle Price** = selling price of the full bundle
- **Total Bundle Cost** = combined cost of all items in the bundle
- **Bundle Profit** = profit earned from the bundle

## Example calculation

If:

- Bundle price = **1200**
- Total bundle cost = **700**

Then:

- Bundle profit = **1200 - 700**
- Bundle profit = **500**
- Bundle margin = **41.67%**

## What is bundle profit?

Bundle profit is the amount left after subtracting the combined cost of all bundle items from the selling price.

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- forgetting all component costs
- using the wrong selling price
- ignoring packaging or fulfilment costs
- focusing only on revenue instead of margin

## Bundle profit vs bundle price

These are closely related.

- **Bundle price** shows what the customer pays
- **Bundle profit** shows what the business earns before overhead
