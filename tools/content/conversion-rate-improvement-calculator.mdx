Use this calculator to measure the improvement between an old and new conversion rate.

## What this calculator does

The conversion rate improvement calculator shows the percentage uplift between two conversion rates.

It is useful for:

- CRO reporting
- A/B test analysis
- landing page reviews
- performance benchmarking

## Improvement Formula

**Improvement % = ((New Rate − Old Rate) ÷ Old Rate) × 100**

Where:

- **Old Rate** = previous conversion rate
- **New Rate** = updated conversion rate
- **Improvement %** = percentage increase or decrease

## Example calculation

If:

- Old conversion rate = **2%**
- New conversion rate = **3%**

Then:

- Improvement = **((3 − 2) ÷ 2) × 100**
- Improvement = **50%**

## What is conversion rate improvement?

Conversion rate improvement measures how much better or worse a new conversion rate is compared with the old one.

It helps show whether optimization work is actually making a difference.

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- confusing percentage points with percentage improvement
- comparing rates without enough sample size
- overstating small changes without context

## Conversion rate vs conversion rate improvement

These are different ideas.

- **Conversion rate** is the percentage of visitors who convert
- **Conversion rate improvement** is the percentage change between two conversion rates
