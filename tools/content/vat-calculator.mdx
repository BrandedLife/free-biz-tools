Use this calculator to estimate VAT added to a price or included in a price.

## What this calculator does

The VAT calculator helps you determine:

- VAT amount
- price including VAT
- price excluding VAT

## VAT Formula

**VAT Amount = Price × VAT Rate**

## Example calculation

If:

- Price = **100**
- VAT rate = **15%**

Then:

VAT = **15**

Total price = **115**

## What is VAT?

Value Added Tax (VAT) is a consumption tax applied to goods and services.

Many countries apply VAT at different rates.

## Why VAT matters

VAT calculations help businesses:

- comply with tax regulations
- price products correctly
- manage accounting records
