Use this calculator to estimate VAT amounts and compare VAT-inclusive and VAT-exclusive pricing.

## Formula

**VAT Amount = Base Amount × VAT %**

**Including VAT = Base Amount + VAT Amount**

## Example

If the base amount is **1000** and VAT is **15%**:

- VAT Amount = 150
- Including VAT = 1150

## Why it matters

VAT affects customer pricing, bookkeeping, and profit margins. This calculator helps you handle tax-inclusive pricing more accurately.

## Common use cases

- Product pricing
- Invoicing
- Quotation building
- Ecommerce tax checks
