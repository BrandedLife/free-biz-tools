Use this calculator to estimate Shopify-style payment fees and your net revenue after transaction costs.

## Formula

**Fee = (Sale Amount × Fee %) + Fixed Fee**

**Net Amount = Sale Amount - Fee**

## Example

If the sale amount is **1000**, the fee is **2.9%**, and the fixed fee is **0.30**:

- Fee = 29.30
- Net Amount = 970.70

## Why it matters

Payment processing fees reduce your actual revenue. This calculator helps you price products more accurately.

## Common use cases

- Store pricing reviews
- Profit checks
- Payment cost planning
- Comparing processors
