Use this calculator to estimate landing page conversion rate.

## What this calculator does

The landing page conversion calculator helps measure how many visitors convert on a specific landing page.

It is useful for:

- page testing
- ad campaign analysis
- CRO planning
- landing page optimisation

## Formula

**Landing Page Conversion Rate = Landing Page Conversions ÷ Landing Page Visitors × 100**

Where:

- **Landing Page Conversions** = actions completed on the landing page
- **Landing Page Visitors** = total visitors to the landing page
- **Landing Page Conversion Rate** = percentage of visitors who convert

## Example calculation

If:

- Landing page conversions = **75**
- Landing page visitors = **1500**

Then:

- Landing page conversion rate = **75 ÷ 1500 × 100**
- Landing page conversion rate = **5%**

## What is landing page conversion rate?

Landing page conversion rate is the percentage of visitors who complete a desired action on a landing page.

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- counting the wrong conversion event
- using incomplete visitor data
- mixing traffic sources
- comparing pages with very different intent

## Landing page conversion rate vs CTR

These are closely related.

- **Landing page conversion rate** measures what happens after the click
- **CTR** measures whether people click the ad or link in the first place
