## What is amortization?

Amortization is the process of paying off a loan over time through regular payments.

Each payment usually includes:

- interest
- principal repayment

## Why amortization matters

Amortization helps borrowers:

- understand loan affordability
- estimate total repayment cost
- compare different loan terms
- plan long-term financing

## When to use this calculator

Use this calculator when you want to:

- estimate monthly payments

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- overlooking total cost over the life of the loan
- focusing only on monthly payment
- ignoring rate changes on variable loans
- forgetting fees and insurance

## Amortization vs loan interest

These are related but different.

- **Amortization** looks at the repayment structure over time
- **Loan interest** looks more directly at borrowing cost
