## What this calculator does

The monthly growth rate calculator helps estimate the percentage growth between a starting value and an ending value over one month.

It is useful for:

- growth tracking
- revenue analysis
- SaaS reporting
- performance monitoring

## Formula

**Monthly Growth Rate = (End Value - Start Value) ÷ Start Value × 100**

Where:

- **Start Value** = value at the beginning of the month
- **End Value** = value at the end of the month
- **Monthly Growth Rate** = percentage increase or decrease over the month

## Example calculation

If:

- Start value = **1000**
- End value = **1200**

Then:

- Monthly growth rate = **(1200 - 1000) ÷ 1000 × 100**
- Monthly growth rate = **20%**

## What is monthly growth rate?

Monthly growth rate shows how quickly a metric such as revenue, users, or customers changed over a month.

## Why monthly growth rate matters

This calculation helps businesses:

- track short-term momentum
- compare monthly performance
- support forecasting
- spot trend changes early

## When to use this calculator

Use this calculator when you want to:

- measure month-to-month change
- review growth trends
- benchmark campaigns or business periods
- support reporting

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- comparing mismatched periods
- using zero or near-zero starting values carelessly
- confusing growth amount with growth rate
- ignoring seasonality

## Monthly growth rate vs annual growth rate

These are closely related.

- **Monthly growth rate** tracks short-term percentage change
- **Annual growth rate** smooths growth over a full year or more
