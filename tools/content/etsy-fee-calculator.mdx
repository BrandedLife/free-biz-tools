## What this calculator does

The Etsy fee calculator helps estimate total Etsy fees and your net amount after those fees.

It is useful for:

- Etsy pricing
- margin analysis
- payout estimation
- product planning

## Formula

**Etsy Fee = Listing Fee + (Sale Price × Transaction Fee Percentage)**

Where:

- **Listing Fee** = fixed listing cost
- **Sale Price** = product sale amount
- **Transaction Fee Percentage** = Etsy transaction fee rate
- **Etsy Fee** = estimated total Etsy fee

## Example calculation

If:

- Sale price = **800**
- Listing fee = **10**
- Transaction fee = **6.5%**

Then:

- Etsy fee = **10 + (800 × 0.065)**
- Etsy fee = **62**
- Net amount = **738**

## What are Etsy fees?

Etsy fees are the platform costs charged when you list and sell products on Etsy.

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- leaving out the listing fee
- ignoring payment processing costs
- using the wrong fee percentage
- pricing too tightly around gross revenue

## Etsy fees vs marketplace fees

These are closely related.

- **Etsy fees** are Etsy-specific
- **Marketplace fees** are broader platform commission estimates
