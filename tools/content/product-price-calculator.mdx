Use this calculator to estimate a product selling price based on cost, markup, VAT, and fees.

## Formula

**Base Selling Price = Cost × (1 + Markup %)**

**Fee Adjustment = Base Selling Price × Fee %**

**Final Price Ex VAT = Base Selling Price + Fee Adjustment**

**Final Price Inc VAT = Final Price Ex VAT + VAT**

## Example

If product cost is **100**, markup is **50%**, fees are **3%**, and VAT is **15%**:

- Price Ex VAT = 154.50
- VAT = 23.18
- Price Inc VAT = 177.68

## Why it matters

A strong pricing decision must include markup, tax, and payment costs. This calculator helps avoid underpricing.

## Common use cases

- Product launch pricing
- Ecommerce margin planning
- Wholesale and retail pricing
- Channel fee adjustments
