Use this calculator to estimate remarketing ROI.

## What this calculator does

The remarketing ROI calculator helps measure the return generated by remarketing campaigns.

It is useful for:

- campaign review
- budget planning
- performance analysis
- marketing optimisation

## Formula

**Remarketing ROI = (Remarketing Revenue - Remarketing Cost) ÷ Remarketing Cost × 100**

Where:

- **Remarketing Revenue** = revenue attributed to remarketing
- **Remarketing Cost** = total remarketing spend
- **Remarketing ROI** = percentage return from the campaign

## Example calculation

If:

- Remarketing revenue = **12000**
- Remarketing cost = **3000**

Then:

- Remarketing ROI = **(12000 - 3000) ÷ 3000 × 100**
- Remarketing ROI = **300%**

## What is remarketing ROI?

Remarketing ROI shows how efficiently your remarketing spend is turning into revenue.

## Why remarketing ROI matters

This calculation helps businesses:

- judge campaign efficiency
- compare remarketing against other channels
- support budget decisions
- improve profitability

## When to use this calculator

Use this calculator when you want to:

- measure campaign returns
- review retargeting performance
- justify ad spend

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- over-attributing revenue
- ignoring total remarketing costs
- mixing time periods
- comparing campaigns with different attribution windows

## Remarketing ROI vs ROAS

These are closely related.

- **Remarketing ROI** focuses on profit relative to cost
- **ROAS** focuses on revenue relative to ad spend
