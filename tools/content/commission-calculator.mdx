## What this calculator does

The commission calculator shows how much commission is earned from a sale based on the sales amount and commission percentage.

It is useful for:

- sales compensation planning
- commission checks
- forecasting earnings
- incentive reviews

## Commission Formula

**Commission = Sales Amount × Commission Rate**

Where:

- **Sales Amount** = value of the sale
- **Commission Rate** = percentage paid as commission
- **Commission** = amount earned

## Example calculation

If:

- Sales amount = **10000**
- Commission rate = **5%**

Then:

- Commission = **10000 × 0.05**
- Commission = **500**

## What is commission?

Commission is a payment earned based on a percentage of sales or revenue.

It is common in sales, affiliate programs, and performance-based compensation structures.

## Why commission matters

Commission helps businesses and individuals:

- forecast earnings
- design incentives
- understand payout structures
- compare sales performance

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- using the wrong percentage
- confusing commission rate with profit margin
- forgetting tiered or variable commission structures

## Commission vs percentage

These are related but different.

- **Percentage** is the general proportion
- **Commission** is a specific payment calculated from a percentage of sales
