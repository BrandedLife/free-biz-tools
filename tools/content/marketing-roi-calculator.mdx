Use this calculator to estimate marketing ROI based on revenue generated and marketing costs.

## What this calculator does

The marketing ROI calculator shows how effectively marketing spend generates returns.

It is useful for:

- campaign analysis
- budget planning
- channel comparison
- performance reporting

## Marketing ROI Formula

**Marketing ROI = ((Revenue − Cost) ÷ Cost) × 100**

Where:

- **Revenue** = income generated from marketing
- **Cost** = total marketing spend
- **Marketing ROI** = percentage return on marketing investment

## Example calculation

If:

- Revenue = **5000**
- Marketing cost = **1000**

Then:

- Marketing ROI = **((5000 − 1000) ÷ 1000) × 100**
- Marketing ROI = **400%**

## What is marketing ROI?

Marketing ROI measures how much return is generated from marketing spend.

It helps determine whether campaigns and channels are delivering worthwhile outcomes.

## Why marketing ROI matters

Marketing ROI helps businesses:

- allocate budgets better
- compare channels and campaigns
- improve performance
- justify marketing spend

- review campaign profitability

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- excluding some campaign costs
- overstating attributed revenue
- using inconsistent time periods
- confusing ROI with ROAS

## Marketing ROI vs ROAS

These are related but different.

- **Marketing ROI** compares profit-like return to cost
- **ROAS** compares revenue to ad spend

ROI gives a broader profitability view, while ROAS is often more channel-specific.
