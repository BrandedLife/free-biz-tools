## What this calculator does

The fulfillment cost calculator helps estimate total cost of fulfilling orders.

It is useful for:

- operations analysis
- fulfilment planning
- cost forecasting
- channel comparison

## Formula

**Fulfillment Cost = Orders Fulfilled × Cost Per Order**

Where:

- **Orders Fulfilled** = total orders processed and shipped
- **Cost Per Order** = average fulfilment cost per order
- **Fulfillment Cost** = total fulfilment cost

## Example calculation

If:

- Orders fulfilled = **2000**
- Cost per order = **18**

Then:

- Fulfillment cost = **2000 × 18**
- Fulfillment cost = **36000**

## What is fulfillment cost?

Fulfillment cost is the total operational cost of processing and shipping orders.

## Why fulfillment cost matters

This calculation helps businesses:

- understand fulfilment expense
- improve operational efficiency
- compare partners or channels
- support pricing decisions

## When to use this calculator

Use this calculator when you want to:

- forecast fulfilment spend
- compare providers
- review operating efficiency
- plan order volume growth

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- leaving out packaging or labour
- using inconsistent order volumes
- ignoring outsourced fees
- comparing providers without the same service level

## Fulfillment cost vs pick and pack cost

These are closely related.

- **Fulfillment cost** is broader total processing cost
- **Pick and pack cost** focuses specifically on picking and packing activity
