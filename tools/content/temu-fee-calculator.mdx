## What this calculator does

The Temu fee calculator helps estimate platform fees and the amount left after those fees.

It is useful for:

- channel evaluation
- pricing analysis
- margin planning
- payout forecasting

## Formula

**Temu Fee = Sale Price × Fee Percentage**

Where:

- **Sale Price** = total sale amount
- **Fee Percentage** = Temu fee rate
- **Temu Fee** = estimated platform fee

## Example calculation

If:

- Sale price = **900**
- Temu fee = **10%**

Then:

- Temu fee = **900 × 0.10**
- Temu fee = **90**
- Net amount = **810**

## What are Temu fees?

Temu fees are the costs charged by the platform on each sale.

Common mistakes include:

- using incomplete fee inputs
- comparing gross revenue instead of net revenue
- ignoring non-platform costs
- forgetting shipping implications
