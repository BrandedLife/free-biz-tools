## What this calculator does

The loan payment calculator helps you estimate how much you will pay each month on a loan.

It considers:

- loan amount
- interest rate
- loan term

This helps you plan repayments before committing to a loan.

## Loan Payment Formula

Monthly loan payments are calculated using the amortization formula.

The formula considers:

- principal amount
- interest rate
- loan duration

## What is a loan payment?

A loan payment is the regular amount paid to repay borrowed money.

Payments typically include:

- principal repayment
- interest charges

## Why loan calculations matter

Loan calculations help you:

- plan monthly budgets

- avoid borrowing more than you can afford
- understand total interest costs

## Common mistakes

Common mistakes include:

- ignoring interest rates
- overlooking loan fees
- misunderstanding loan terms
- assuming fixed payments when interest rates vary

## Loan payment vs total loan cost

Monthly payment shows what you pay each month.

Total loan cost shows how much you repay including interest.

Understanding both helps you make better borrowing decisions.

## FAQs

### How do you calculate loan payments?

Loan payments are calculated using loan amount, interest rate, and loan term.

### What affects monthly loan payments?

Loan size, interest rate, and repayment duration affect the payment amount.

### Why should you estimate loan payments?

### Can interest rates change loan payments?

Yes, higher interest rates increase monthly payments.
